Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef Stroganoff is a creamy and savory dish made with tender strips of beef, mushrooms, onions, and a rich sour cream sauce. It's typically served over egg noodles or rice

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Beef broth
  • Sour cream
  • Flour
  • Butter

Steps

  • Saut√© beef strips in butter until browned.
  • Add onions, garlic, and mushrooms; cook until softened.
  • Stir in flour, then add beef broth and simmer until thickened.
  • Mix in sour cream and season with salt and pepper.
  • Serve over egg noodles or rice.
