Chicken Parmesan

Description

Breaded chicken breasts topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, often served over pasta.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzzarella cheese
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Olive oil

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Coat chicken breasts in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and cover with a breadcrumb and Parmesan mixture.
  • Cook chicken in olive oil until golden brown.
  • Place chicken in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  • Bake until chicken is cooked through and cheese is melted.
