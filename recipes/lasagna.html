Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers wide pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey mozzarella cheese. This hearty and comforting meal is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions. The flavors meld together as the dish bakes, creating a deliciously satisfying and flavorful experience in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ricott cheese
  • Mozzerella cheese

Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°)
  • Brown ground beef with onion and garlic; add tomato sauce and simmer.
  • Mix ricotta cheese with egg.
  • Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  • Layer meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes; remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  • Cool for 10 minutes before serving
